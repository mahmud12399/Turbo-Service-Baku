How Turbochargers Work

Understanding the science behind forced induction

1

Exhaust Gas Energy

Hot exhaust gases from the engine spin the turbine wheel at extremely high speeds, typically 100,000+ RPM.

2

Compressor Action

The turbine wheel drives the compressor wheel, which draws in and compresses ambient air.

3

Forced Induction

Compressed air is forced into the engine, allowing more fuel to be burned and increasing power output.
